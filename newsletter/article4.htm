Live From Mac Expo 2005 in London

November 19th, 2008 - Posted by: Andrew Shacknew

Fresh back from this week’s MacExpo 2005 in London, U.K., iLounge Contributing Editor and Chief Forum Administrator Bob Levens has filed this report on what was hot and not at the show.

Mac World in London was very exciting. Apple had tons of new products on display in one of the best product showcases ever. People were lined up at pretty much every booth waiting to try products that were shown at the expo during Steve Job's speech.
