Successful launch!

November 19th, 2008

Welcome to iNewsletter and thanks for being patient for the launch of the site. The purpose of the site is to provide lots of information for one of the leading products in the music industry - the iPod. We are going to have tutorials, news and a bunch of content for you to browse through on the site.

Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide information to Apple or iPod fans. This magazine is designed for iPod users.

Separate Sections

We have different sections for our news and content. If you want to learn about the latest and greatest you should check out the News section on the site or you could just go to the Article section to look up tutorials on how to operate the iPod.

There are two sections in total on the site that you will frequent often, the two sections are:

  • News - Lots of new information
  • Content - Tutorials + Help
