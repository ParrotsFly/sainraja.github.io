About us

We are a group of college students who love the iPOD and we love music! So we decided to start a website where we can share our passion with others.

iNewsLetter is an online newsletter or magazine. The purpose of the site is to help people with iPODs get the most out of their player. We have different types of content available on our site that will help iPOD users. We have tutorials and various guides that we put up weekly that makes sure they are kept up to date. We also post news article talking about the latest iPOD coming out by Apple.
