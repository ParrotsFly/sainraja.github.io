My IPod's click wheel freezes? How do I fix it?

To fix it you need to hold the select and play button for a couple seconds and perform a quick reformat. You can also visit the official support site provided by Apple to get more help. You can visit their site by clicking here.

How do I Add or Remove a song from the IPod?

You add songs by searching for a file/folder using iTunes' file menu. You remove songs by simply connectiong your iPOD to the computer and removing the song you don't want from the library and update the iPod. Make sure the IPOD is synced with the computer. If you just purchased an iPod - you need to install the driver for Windows and just plug it in for Macintosh. If you are on Windows, do not connect the iPod directly to the computer, you need to wait till th software instructs you to connect it.

Do I have to use the iTunes software with IPod?

iTunes is required to transfer songs to and from the iPod. iTunes converts the songs you have on your computer and allows you to buy songs through the online store for about a dollar. Most songs that are on iTunes can be bought without DRM. If you don't have iTunes on your computer already you can get it by going to Apple's website.

How can I get my battery replaced?

To get the battery on the iPod unit replaced you have to go to Apple's website and fill in a form and mail in the unit.

How long does it take to charge the battery of an IPod?

A full charge takes about 4 to 5 hours.
