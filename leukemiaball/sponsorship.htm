The Leukemia Ball has become one of the most successful charitable events in Washington because of the generosity of its sponsors. Each year, numerous corporations and individuals become sponsors of the Ball at a variety of different levels. We invite you to learn about these various levels of sponsorship by exploring the links listed above.

To become a sponsor, please complete the form below and then press the "Submit" button at the bottom of the page or download the Sponsorship Form. After your information has been received, a representative from The Leukemia & Lymphoma Society will contact you shortly.

If you have any questions regarding sponsorship opportunities, please contact Kristy Kent at The Leukemia Lymphoma Society, (703) 960-1100 ext. 230 or kristy.kent@lls.org.
