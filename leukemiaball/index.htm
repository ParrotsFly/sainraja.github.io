Save Saturday, March 29, 2008 on your calendar! That is the date of next year's ball.

Welcome to the Leukemia Ball's website:

Since 1988, millions of dollars have been raised for the fight against leukemia, lymphoma, Hodgkin's disease and myeloma through our annual Leukemia Ball. Each year, dedicated volunteers have exceeded all goals, making this one of the largest charitable events in Washington. The Leukemia & Lymphoma Society is honored to be associated with so many exceptional and caring volunteers.
