Presenting

The Leukemia & Lymphoma Society seeks to partner with a leading organization in presenting the Leukemia Ball. Presenting sponsorship of this prestigious event is an opportunity to not only support the Society in its mission to find cures for blood cancers, but to be affiliated with one of the nation's premier special events.

The Leukemia Ball means business. Our corporate sponsors find a four-fold benefit to participating in the Ball.

  • An evening to remember for their executive clients and staff.
  • Association with the largest non-political black-tie event in Washington, and the largest nationwide dedicated to fighting blood cancer. Leukemia is the number one cancer killer of children and young adults under the age of 20.

  • Tremendous visibility prior to, and at, one of the largest concentrated gatherings of Washington’s business elite. Visibility is gained throughout the evening with significant recognition of your company and its high level of contribution, as well as by the personal attendance of your company’s representatives.

  • Ascending levels of significant exposure of your company as a true, good citizen in the broader corporate community.

And, should you choose to become a Presenting Sponsor and participate in this worthwhile event, your visibility would reach every participating business and supporter as event promotion and publicity rolls out during the year.
