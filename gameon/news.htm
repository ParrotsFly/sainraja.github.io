Star Ocean is going to have three discs

December 4th, 2008

Anyone familiar with Japanese RPG's during the PS1 era should be familiar with the term disc swapping. At that time, people looked at that as a good thing. It meant the game was going to be longer than other traditional games. Developers don't have many reasons to use more than one disc this generation but JRPG games are the only type of games that require more.

Xbox 360 NXE release

December 3rd, 2008

A new update was released last month for Xbox 360 owners. It comes with an interface overhaul called the NXE (New Xbox Experience) that allowers gamers to pick an avatar, install games to their HDD and use a party chat feature or lobby as usually called in games. NXE is Microsoft's answer to the Wii avatar system and PlayStation Home which is set to release this month.
