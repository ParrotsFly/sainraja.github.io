Uncharted: Drake's Fortune Preview

October 15th, 2008

There haven't been many games out there like Uncharted: Drake's fortune. This is a game which really makes you feel like you are playing a movie. The characters, the voice acting are done very well. This game came out last year on November the 20th.

Resistance 2

October 16th, 2008

Resistance 2 will be releasing in United States on Novemeber 4th, 2008. It is a first person shooter game which will have eight player co-op mode and 15 hour campaign mode. The game also has access to the PlayStation Network allowing players to connect online and play others in various matches.

Prince of Persia

October 16th, 2008

Prince of Persia is getting a complete revamp with a new story. The game won't be continuing from where the Two Thrones left off. We have a new prince, new art style. Everything in the game has changed. You have to work with another AI character in the game, similar to how you had to work with Farah in the Sands of Time but your AI partner will help you more compared to before. She will help you with platforming, fighting etc.
