Mission Statement

Art of Islam is an online website offering calligraphic art for people who appreciate art unlike competing sites, which display art but don’t describe its importance to the user. Art of Islam will display different types of art form used in Islam and have a detailed description next to every piece displayed online.

Art of Islam is a website that focuses greatly on Islamic Art. The site is going to provide users with information regarding Islamic art and the history behind it. Users are going to learn to appreciate Islamic art for what it is.

Targeted Message

The target message of the site is to inform people that Islamic Art is just as modern and unique as Western Art. The users of the site will gain knowledge about Islam’s artistic side.

Site Goal

The purpose of the site is to provide information on Islamic Calligraphy, giving people a better idea of the unique style of writing.

Why is the name of the site Art of Islam?
