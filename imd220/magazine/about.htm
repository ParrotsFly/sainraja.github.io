About the Site

Ashburn Town Magazine is a non-profit magazine, set up to to get the community of Ashburn together. People can use this site to get the latest news surrouding Ashburn and the county is it located in. The magazine has pictures of the town, news articles and information that the people living in Ashburn or moving to Ashburn can benefit from.

Why do we provide information for free?

We believe that to bring the community together you can't charge them for it. Our number one goal has been to reach the community and encourage everyone to work together to improve Ashburn. The magazine makes revenue through ads the user clicks on.
