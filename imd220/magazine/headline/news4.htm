Sweetwater Tavern and Clyde’s Two Busiest Restaurants In Loudoun County, Virginia
February 20, 2007

Sweetwater Tavern in Sterling, Virginia has the distinct honor of being the busiest restaurant in Loudoun County. But Clyde’s at Willow Creek Farm, which just opened in Ashburn (Broadlands), is already number 2 and catching up to Sweetwater Tavern quickly.

And rumor has it that Clyde’s has the record for the largest liqour and food order in Loudoun County history. With over 550 seats and 4 bars, the rumor’s probably true. Can you imagine the cost of those two orders?!
