Brambleton Town Center Update
March 7, 2007

The first Barnes & Noble in Loudoun County is coming soon to the Brambleton Town Center. And Fox Cinemas is scheduled to open in May 2007. Recent Openings in Phase I of the Brambleton Town Center…La Belle Nail Spa and A&A Music “Where Your Love for Music is Discovered”. A&A Music offer music lessons (including singing), music instruments, rentals, gifts, sheet music, etc. Opening Soon in Phase I…Nick’s Corner Grill, Froots, Edible Arrangements, Animal Hospital and My Thai Place. Coming Soon to Phase II…Barnes & Noble, The Papery, Cradle & Crayon, Classic Closets by Design, Edibles Incredible, The Nest Egg. For more information, check out www.brambletonresidents.com.
